Events

GraphiCon

GraphiCon is an exciting event for Adobe Photoshop enthusiasts, offering a platform to showcase creative skills in digital art, photo manipulation, and graphic design through engaging challenges and competitions.

Dev.py

Dev.py is a dynamic event for Python enthusiasts, where participants tackle coding challenges, showcase innovative projects, and enhance their programming skills in a competitive and collaborative environment.

Enigma

Enigma is a thrilling cryptic hunt that tests participants' problem-solving skills and intellect, as they decipher clues, unravel mysteries, and race against time to crack challenging puzzles.

3DVerse

3DVerse is an exciting event for Blender enthusiasts, where participants explore 3D modeling, animation, and design, showcasing their creativity and technical skills through immersive challenges and competitions.

Webforge

Webforge is an engaging event for web development enthusiasts, where participants create innovative websites, tackle coding challenges, and showcase their front-end and back-end skills in a competitive environment.

Maths Mania

The event includes a bingo-based prelim round with practical problem-solving, followed by a crossword final centered on mathematical history and contributions of renowned mathematicians.

Eureka

Two scientist names will be given to each team of 2 participants. After researching, the team needs to devise a combined theory from theories given by the scientists. Debate round will be held.

Ideation

Based on shark tank, participants need to present a working model of a product that would thrive in the current market. For prelims, ppt needs to be submitted showcasing the idea.

Scicathon

Trivia and quiz event based on physics and chemistry course of class 11-12 and their real life application.

Number Nerds

Number Nerds is a competitive event for Class XI-XII teams, featuring 4 rounds and a bonus round, where participants tackle syllabus-based questions, scoring based on speed and accuracy.

Mathsquerade

Mathsquerade challenges Class IX-X teams to bring math concepts to life in 3-5 minutes, blending creativity, clarity, and real-world applications for a dynamic performance.
